## üöÄ How to Play

1. **Enable Audio**: Click anywhere to enable sound effects and Game of Thrones music
2. **Move**: Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons
3. **Jump**: Spacebar, Up arrow, or jump button
4. **Collect Power-ups**: Mushrooms grow Mario, fire flowers give fire powers
5. **Fight Enemies**: Jump on enemies or use fireballs
6. **Reach the Boss**: Survive to x=1200 for the Bowser encounter
7. **Explore**: Keep going right for infinite procedurally generated content
